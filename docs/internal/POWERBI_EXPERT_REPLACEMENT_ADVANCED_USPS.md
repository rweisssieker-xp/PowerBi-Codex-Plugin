## Strategic Effect

- Marketplaces create reusable governed assets instead of one-off reports.
- Refactoring, consolidation, and retirement reduce legacy report debt.
- Lineage, evidence, and certification make AI-generated BI auditable.
- Self-healing refresh and cost-to-value optimization make operations measurable.
- Digital twin, action learning, and boardroom autopilot connect analytics to decisions.
