# Lead2Order Process Owner Analytics

## en-US

### Purpose

Provide the Lead2Order Process Owner with all relevant Power BI evaluations to manage lead flow, conversion, speed, quality, revenue leakage, owner accountability, and improvement actions from initial lead to booked order.

### Process Scope

Lead2Order covers Lead Intake, Qualification, MQL, SQL, Opportunity, Quote, Order Creation, Lost/Disqualified outcomes, and exception handling. The process owner needs cross-functional visibility across marketing, sales development, account executives, sales operations, product, pricing, and order management.

### Required Evaluations

| Evaluation | Business question | Primary KPIs | Main slices |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Executive Overview | Are we creating enough qualified demand and converting it into orders? | Lead Volume, Pipeline Value, Order Value, Order Conversion Rate, Win Rate | Region, channel, product, segment |
| Funnel Conversion | Where do leads drop out between intake and order? | MQL Rate, SQL Rate, Opportunity Conversion Rate, Quote Conversion Rate, Order Conversion Rate | Stage, source, campaign, owner |
| Lead Quality | Which sources create leads that actually become opportunities and orders? | Qualified Lead Rate, Data Quality Issue Rate, Disqualification Rate | Channel, campaign, account type |
| Response SLA | Are teams reacting quickly enough to new leads? | Lead Response SLA %, Median Response Time, Stale Lead Count | Owner, team, region, priority |
| Stage Aging | Where does the process slow down? | Lead-to-Order Cycle Time, Stage Aging Days, Handoff Breach Count | Stage, owner, product, region |
| Opportunity Performance | Which opportunities create value and which get stuck? | Pipeline Value, Weighted Pipeline, Opportunity Win Rate, Lost Value | Sales owner, product, segment |
| Quote Performance | Are quotes timely, complete, and converted? | Quote Conversion Rate, Quote Cycle Time, Discount Leakage, Quote Rework Count | Product, pricing owner, customer |
| Lost Reason and Leakage | Why do we lose demand before order? | Lost Value, Top Lost Reasons, Competitor Loss Rate, No-Decision Rate | Lost reason, competitor, segment |
| Campaign ROI | Which campaigns create actual orders, not just leads? | Campaign ROI, Cost per Qualified Lead, Cost per Order | Campaign, channel, quarter |
| Action Cockpit | What must be fixed this week? | Exception Backlog, Stale Leads, SLA Breaches, High-Value Leakage | Owner, due date, severity |
| Data Quality | Can the process be trusted analytically? | Missing Owner Rate, Missing Source Rate, Invalid Stage Rate, Duplicate Lead Rate | Source system, field, owner |

### Report Pages

1. Executive Overview: funnel totals, conversion, pipeline, orders, leakage, SLA, top actions.
2. Funnel Conversion: stage-by-stage conversion and loss points.
3. Lead Quality and Source ROI: campaign/channel quality and financial return.
4. Response SLA and Stage Aging: delays, aging distribution, breached handoffs.
5. Opportunity and Quote Performance: pipeline, quotes, win/loss, discount and rework.
6. Lost Reason and Leakage: lost value by reason, competitor, segment, product.
7. Sales Owner Action Cockpit: owner-specific exceptions, stale items, next-best actions.
8. Data Quality and Governance: missing fields, duplicate leads, invalid states, source coverage.

### Owner Decisions Enabled

- Reallocate budget from low-quality lead sources to high-conversion channels.
- Escalate SLA breaches by sales owner, region, or team.
- Remove bottlenecks in qualification, quoting, pricing, or order creation.
- Prioritize high-value stale leads and opportunities.
- Align marketing and sales on one accepted funnel definition.
- Approve KPI contracts and data-quality blocking rules before release.
