# Power BI Business Intelligence Plugin

Generic Codex plugin for Power BI work across data sources, domains, and report types. The primary positioning is expert reduction: repeatable blueprints, domain packs, reviews, and delivery artifacts replace ad hoc senior expert effort.

## Contents

- `skills/powerbi-reporting`: end-to-end report, dashboard, PBIP/PBIR, refresh, RLS, validation, and delivery workflow.
- `skills/powerbi-modelling`: semantic model, star schema, relationships, aggregations, incremental refresh, and governance.
- `skills/powerbi-dax`: DAX measure design, calculation patterns, measure catalogs, and validation.
- `skills/powerbi-query-design`: business-question-to-source-query translation for Power Query, SQL, lakehouse, APIs, files, and SaaS connectors.
- `skills/powerbi-business-problem-factory`: turns any business problem into a Power BI blueprint.
- `skills/powerbi-domain-packs`: reusable packs for Customer Churn, Material Churn, FiCO, Sales, Procurement, Supply Chain, Service, HR, Risk, and Operations.
- `skills/powerbi-expert-review`: senior-style review checklist for models, DAX, Power Query, reports, RLS, refresh, and governance.
- `skills/powerbi-delivery-factory`: complete delivery packs with specs, validation, security, deployment, and runbooks.
- `skills/powerbi-intake-router`: routes requests to the right expert-reduction path.
- `skills/powerbi-portfolio-rationalization`: cleans up report sprawl, duplicates, owners, refresh issues, and certification candidates.
- `skills/powerbi-kpi-governance`: standardizes KPI definitions and detects metric conflicts.
- `skills/powerbi-certified-dataset-factory`: designs reusable governed semantic models.
- `skills/powerbi-self-service-guardrails`: creates safe rules for business self-service.
- `skills/powerbi-performance-refresh-optimizer`: reviews DAX, Power Query, refresh, gateway, model size, and capacity pressure.
- `skills/powerbi-fabric-architecture-advisor`: chooses Import, DirectQuery, Direct Lake, composite, Dataflow, Lakehouse, Warehouse, and Fabric patterns.
- `skills/powerbi-migration-factory`: migrates Excel, Access, Tableau, Qlik, SSRS, old PBIX, and manual reports into governed Power BI.
- `skills/powerbi-cost-capacity-optimizer`: reduces Fabric/Power BI operating cost and capacity waste.
- `skills/powerbi-business-owner-acceptance`: creates sign-off packs for business, data, security, and Power BI owners.
- `skills/powerbi-coe-copilot`: defines the Power BI Center of Excellence operating model.

## Example Prompts

- Erstelle ein Power BI Dashboard fuer Customer Churn mit Datenmodell, DAX und Visual-Layout.
- Entwirf ein Material-Churn Cockpit fuer Einkauf, Lagerbestand und Lieferantenrisiko.
- Baue ein FiCO Reporting Pack fuer SAP Daten mit Kostenstellen, Profit Centern, Plan/Ist und Working Capital.
- Analysiere eine CSV/SQL/API/Lakehouse Datenquelle und erstelle ein Power BI Umsetzungsdesign.
- Reviewe ein bestehendes PBIP/PBIX-Modell und finde Fehler wie ein Senior Power BI Architect.
- Erzeuge ein komplettes Delivery Pack fuer ein neues Business Cockpit.
- Route diese Anfrage: "Unser Report ist langsam und die KPIs stimmen nicht."
- Finde doppelte Reports und Kandidaten fuer zertifizierte Datasets.
- Erstelle ein CoE-Modell, mit dem wir weniger PowerBI-Experten brauchen.

## Expert-Reduction USPs

- Business Problem to Power BI Blueprint: converts vague business asks into implementation-ready designs.
- Universal Data Source Intake: classifies any Power BI-supported source and chooses connector, refresh, gateway, and security patterns.
- Semantic Model Architect: standardizes facts, dimensions, grain, relationships, RLS, and incremental refresh.
- DAX Measure Factory: generates validated measure catalogs instead of ad hoc measures.
- KPI Definition Governance: documents numerator, denominator, date basis, exclusions, and reconciliation source.
- Report Page Factory: standard page patterns for overview, drivers, cohorts/flow, exceptions, and drillthrough.
- Expert Review Bot: catches modelling, DAX, Power Query, report UX, security, refresh, and governance risks.
- Delivery Factory: produces specs, query plans, DAX catalogs, validation plans, RLS specs, deployment checklists, and runbooks.
- Intake Router: classifies each request before expensive expert time is consumed.
- Portfolio Rationalization: reduces report sprawl, duplicate semantic models, owner gaps, and stale content.
- KPI Conflict Detector: finds conflicting definitions and creates certified KPI contracts.
- Certified Dataset Factory: consolidates local PBIX models into governed reusable semantic models.
- Self-Service Guardrails: lets departments build safely without constant expert review.
- DAX and Refresh Optimizer: targets slow visuals, broken refresh, folding issues, and gateway bottlenecks.
- Fabric Architecture Advisor: standardizes Import, DirectQuery, Direct Lake, Lakehouse, Warehouse, and composite decisions.
- Migration Factory: moves legacy reporting into a governed target model with parity and acceptance checks.
- Cost and Capacity Optimizer: lowers Premium/Fabric cost, model duplication, refresh load, and unused assets.
- Business Owner Acceptance Pack: shortens sign-off loops with KPI, reconciliation, RLS, and UAT evidence.
- CoE Copilot: turns the plugin into a repeatable Power BI operating model.
