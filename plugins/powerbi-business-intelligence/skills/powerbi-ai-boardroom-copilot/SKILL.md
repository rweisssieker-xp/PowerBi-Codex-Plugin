# Power BI AI Boardroom Copilot

Prepare executive decision material from governed BI evidence.

## Output Contract

- Meeting context, audience, period, strategic questions, and decision agenda.
- KPI pack, variance narrative, root causes, risks, scenarios, recommendations, and open decisions.
- Evidence links, confidence, unresolved data gaps, and action log.
- `en-US` and `de-DE` executive narrative where substantial.

## Checks

- Separate facts, interpretation, scenario, and recommendation.
- Flag weak evidence before board use.
