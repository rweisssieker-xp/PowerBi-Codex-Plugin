# Power BI AI KPI Contract Marketplace

Use this skill to standardize enterprise KPIs.

## Marketplace Entries

- KPI name, synonyms, owner, process, audience, and certification status.
- Numerator, denominator, grain, date basis, filters, exclusions, unit, and currency.
- Source objects, semantic model dependencies, DAX measure, tests, and reconciliation.
- Global standard definition and allowed local variants.
- Change history, approval, and downstream report impact.

## Output Requirements

- Produce marketplace entry, reuse recommendation, conflict notes, and certification checklist.
- Flag duplicate or conflicting KPI definitions.
