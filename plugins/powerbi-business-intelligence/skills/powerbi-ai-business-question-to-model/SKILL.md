# Power BI AI Business Question to Model

Use this skill to convert questions into model requirements.

## Translation Pattern

1. Identify decision, audience, process, grain, and time basis.
2. Identify required facts, dimensions, relationships, and role-playing dates.
3. Identify KPI measures and supporting diagnostics.
4. Identify source-system fields and reconciliation needs.
5. Identify report visuals, drilldowns, and action outputs.

## Output Requirements

- Include model sketch, KPI list, DAX candidates, source requirements, report page plan, and tests.
- Flag questions that cannot be answered with available grain or history.
