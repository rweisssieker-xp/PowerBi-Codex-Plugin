# Power BI Tenant Governance Scanner

Use this skill for tenant-level governance and risk assessment.

## Scan Areas

- Workspaces, owners, roles, inactive workspaces.
- Reports, semantic models, dataflows, apps, endorsements.
- Sharing, export, publish to web, external users.
- Sensitivity labels and protected data.
- Gateways, data sources, credentials, refresh failures.
- Service principals, APIs, deployment pipelines.
- Orphaned assets and missing owners.
- Capacity assignment and usage hotspots.

## Output Requirements

- Produce findings by severity with remediation owner.
- Distinguish tenant policy recommendations from workspace cleanup actions.
