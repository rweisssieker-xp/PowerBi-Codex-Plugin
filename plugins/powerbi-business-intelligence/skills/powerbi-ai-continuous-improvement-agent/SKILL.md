# Power BI AI Continuous Improvement Agent

Turn delivery evidence into better standards and reusable patterns.

## Output Contract

- Input evidence: tickets, defects, reviews, usage, quality gate failures, adoption, and owner feedback.
- Improvement candidates for KPI contracts, semantic models, DAX, Power Query, UX, RLS, docs, and governance.
- Proposed standard update, confidence, affected skills/docs, owner, and rollout plan.
- Measurement: before/after defect rate, expert hours, adoption, and quality score.

## Checks

- Do not change standards from one anecdote.
- Separate proven learning from hypothesis.
