# Power BI AI Report UX Critic

Use this skill before report acceptance or certification.

## Review Areas

- Visual choice matches the business question.
- KPI context, target, trend, variance, and owner are visible.
- Drilldowns and tooltips support root-cause analysis.
- Pages are not overloaded or under-informative.
- Tables are used where detail inspection is needed.
- Accessibility, labels, color usage, and mobile layout are adequate.

## Output Requirements

- Include findings by page and visual, severity, rationale, and recommended redesign.
- Separate cosmetic feedback from decision-risk feedback.
