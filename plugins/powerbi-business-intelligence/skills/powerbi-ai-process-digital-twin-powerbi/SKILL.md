# Power BI AI Process Digital Twin in Power BI

Use this skill for process simulation and scenario analysis.

## Digital Twin Areas

- Event log, process step, duration, status, queue, and handoff modeling.
- Bottleneck, rework, exception, and capacity simulation.
- What-if assumptions and sensitivity.
- Business impact on cash, margin, OTIF, inventory, quality, or service.

## Output Requirements

- Produce digital twin design, assumptions, required data, scenario measures, report pages, and validation limits.
- Mark simulation results as scenario estimates, not observed facts.
