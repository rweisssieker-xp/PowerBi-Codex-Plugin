# Power BI AI Lineage and Blast Radius

Use this skill before changing shared Power BI assets.

## Analysis Areas

- Source-to-M-to-model-to-DAX-to-visual lineage.
- Reports, KPIs, measures, owners, tests, and releases affected by a change.
- Security, RLS/OLS, certification, and refresh impact.
- Required retests and communication plan.

## Output Requirements

- Produce impact graph, affected artifacts, owners, risk, required tests, and mitigation.
- Mark unknown lineage as a governance risk.
