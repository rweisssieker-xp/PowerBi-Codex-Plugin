# Power BI AI Benchmark and Maturity Intelligence

Use this skill for maturity and benchmark assessment.

## Benchmark Areas

- Process KPI performance and benchmark bands.
- Data readiness, source coverage, semantic model maturity, and report maturity.
- Governance, certification, CoE operating model, adoption, and cost maturity.
- Expert-replacement maturity by role and artifact evidence.

## Output Requirements

- Produce maturity score, benchmark comparison, gap list, roadmap, and evidence.
- Mark missing benchmark data or weak comparability explicitly.
