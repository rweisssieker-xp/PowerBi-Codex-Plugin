# Power BI AI KPI Lifecycle Manager

Govern KPIs through their full lifecycle.

## Output Contract

- KPI ID, owner, domain, process, definition, source, measure, reports, and current state.
- Lifecycle action: propose, approve, certify, change, deprecate, retire, replace, or reactivate.
- Impact analysis, communication plan, migration path, and acceptance evidence.
- Version history and audit trail.

## Checks

- Do not retire KPIs without replacement/impact review.
- Require owner sign-off for certified KPI changes.
