# Power BI Training and Enablement Generator

Use this skill to reduce expert dependency through targeted enablement.

## Role Tracks

- Business consumer: navigation, filters, export rules, KPI interpretation.
- Report builder: certified datasets, visual standards, guardrails, quality gates.
- Dataset owner: model ownership, measures, RLS, refresh, certification.
- Reviewer: KPI, DAX, model, security, performance, acceptance checks.
- Admin/CoE: workspace standards, tenant settings, capacity, monitoring, lifecycle.

## Output Requirements

- Produce role, learning objectives, modules, exercises, checklist, and assessment.
- Keep training tied to the organization's actual standards and domain packs.
