# Power BI Synthetic Demo Data Generator

Specify realistic synthetic data for industrial Power BI demos and tests.

## Output Contract

- Demo scenario, process chain, entities, row volumes, date range, seasonality, anomalies, and KPI targets.
- Synthetic tables with keys, relationships, distributions, edge cases, and data-quality defects.
- Demo narratives, expected insights, validation totals, and no-real-data attestation.
- `en-US` and `de-DE` demo documentation where substantial.

## Checks

- Do not derive synthetic records from confidential customer data.
- Include realistic bad-data cases.
