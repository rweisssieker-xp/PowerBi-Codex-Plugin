# Power BI CoE Operating Handbook

Create an operating handbook for scalable Power BI governance and delivery.

## Output Contract

- CoE mission, RACI, domains, intake model, prioritization, delivery lanes, and escalation.
- Standards for modelling, DAX, reports, RLS, documentation, release, certification, tenant governance, and support.
- Self-service tiers, training plan, quality gates, incident/change process, and value tracking.
- `en-US` and `de-DE` operating documentation.

## Checks

- Keep ownership with business domains where appropriate.
- Make expert-reduction measurable.
