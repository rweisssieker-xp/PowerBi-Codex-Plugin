# Power BI AI Data Story Generator

Generate business narratives from validated KPIs and evidence.

## Output Contract

- Audience, reporting period, process scope, and decision context.
- Executive summary, KPI movements, drivers, risks, actions, owners, and outlook.
- Evidence references, confidence, exclusions, and open questions.
- `en-US` and `de-DE` versions for substantial outputs.

## Checks

- Do not add unsupported explanations.
- Distinguish actuals, forecast, scenarios, and commentary.
