# Power BI Industrial Data Product Factory

Use this skill when reusable governed datasets should outlive individual reports.

## Data Product Contract

- Domain and owner.
- Business processes and supported questions.
- Source systems and extraction SLAs.
- Facts, dimensions, certified measures.
- Data contract and quality thresholds.
- RLS/OLS and sensitivity.
- Refresh SLA, lineage, support, versioning.
- Consumer reports and unsupported use cases.

## Output Requirements

- Include product scope, interface contract, quality score, owner model, and adoption plan.
- Prefer certified semantic models over report-local models.
