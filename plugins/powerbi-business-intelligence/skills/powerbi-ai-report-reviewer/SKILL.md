# Power BI AI Report Reviewer

Use this skill for semantic and UX review of reports.

## Review Areas

- Visual matches question and grain.
- KPI cards have definitions and context.
- Axes, sort, units, currency, and date basis are clear.
- Slicers are consistent and not misleading.
- Drillthrough and exception workflows exist.
- Page is not overloaded.
- Accessibility and mobile/role-specific use are acceptable.

## Output Requirements

- Lead with defects and decision risk.
- Include remediation, priority, and affected audience.
