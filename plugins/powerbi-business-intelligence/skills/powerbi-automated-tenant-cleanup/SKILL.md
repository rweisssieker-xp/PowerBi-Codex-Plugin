# Power BI Automated Tenant Cleanup

Create automation-ready tenant cleanup plans.

## Output Contract

- Asset inventory, usage, owner, refresh, endorsement, sharing, labels, gateway, and capacity.
- Findings: orphaned, stale, duplicate, failing, risky, expensive, unmanaged, or retirement candidate.
- Action: assign owner, fix, consolidate, certify, archive, retire candidate, or escalate.
- Approval workflow and rollback plan.

## Checks

- Do not delete without business sign-off.
