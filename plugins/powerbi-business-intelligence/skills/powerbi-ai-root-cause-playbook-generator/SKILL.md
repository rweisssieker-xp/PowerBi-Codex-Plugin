# Power BI AI Root Cause Playbook Generator

Create reusable root-cause playbooks per KPI.

## Output Contract

- KPI, symptom, threshold, segments, owner, and business impact.
- Driver tree, drill path, hypotheses, required visuals, DAX diagnostics, and source fields.
- Standard actions, escalation, retest condition, and evidence.
- Common false causes and data-quality traps.

## Checks

- Playbooks suggest analysis paths; they do not prove causality alone.
