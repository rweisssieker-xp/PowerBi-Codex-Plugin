# Power BI AI Action Effectiveness Learning

Use this skill to close the learning loop after actions.

## Learning Fields

- Recommended action, owner, due date, expected impact, and confidence.
- Whether action was executed, delayed, rejected, or escalated.
- KPI before/after, retest condition, financial impact, and recurrence.
- Patterns that improve future recommendations.

## Output Requirements

- Produce action effectiveness score, lessons learned, recommendation changes, and ROI evidence.
- Do not claim effectiveness without before/after or counterfactual evidence.
