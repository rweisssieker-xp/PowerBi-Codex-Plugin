# Power BI Subscription Revenue Billing Source Pack

## Vendors

Zuora, Chargebee, Stripe Billing, Salesforce Revenue Cloud, SAP BRIM, Oracle Revenue Management, Recurly, BillingPlatform.

## Source Objects

- Account, subscription, contract, plan, amendment, usage, invoice, payment, credit memo, revenue schedule, entitlement.

## Power BI Patterns

- ARR/MRR.
- Net revenue retention and gross revenue retention.
- Churn and expansion.
- Usage to billing reconciliation.
- Revenue recognition.
- DSO and failed payment analysis.

## Output Requirements

- Include subscription lifecycle, amendment logic, billing period, revenue period, usage grain, proration, currency, and recognition rules.
