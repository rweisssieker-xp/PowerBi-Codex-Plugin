# Power BI AI Insight Confidence

Use this skill whenever AI produces an insight, explanation, or recommendation.

## Confidence Factors

- Data coverage and completeness.
- Source freshness and refresh status.
- KPI certification maturity.
- Semantic model validation.
- DAX/Power Query test status.
- RLS/security context.
- Evidence and reconciliation quality.
- Contradictory signals or data gaps.

## Output Requirements

- Score confidence 0-100 with reasons.
- Include blockers and next evidence to raise confidence.
