# Power BI Industry Vertical Packs

Use this skill after the industrial process is known and the company type changes KPI logic, source systems, compliance, or report design.

## Vertical Packs

- Machinery and industrial equipment: engineer-to-order, service margin, installed base, spare parts, project cost, warranty.
- Automotive supplier: program profitability, EDI demand, releases, PPAP, IATF quality, premium freight, production defects.
- Chemicals and process manufacturing: batch, recipe, yield, quality release, energy, emissions, hazardous material, regulatory.
- Pharma and MedTech: batch genealogy, GMP/GxP, validation, complaint, CAPA, traceability, controlled documentation.
- Food and beverage: shelf life, batch, recall, quality hold, demand seasonality, cold chain, waste.
- Electronics: component shortages, lifecycle, yield, RMA, supplier allocation, serial traceability.
- Aerospace and defense: export control, configuration, long-cycle projects, quality escapes, supplier compliance.
- Anlagenbau / engineer-to-order: milestone billing, WBS, engineering change, procurement critical path, claim management.

## Output Requirements

- Include vertical-specific processes, KPIs, source systems, compliance risks, model grains, and report pages.
- State where generic process KPIs must be adapted for industry context.
