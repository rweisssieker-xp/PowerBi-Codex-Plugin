# Power BI AI Owner Acceptance Pack

Use this skill before handover or certification.

## Acceptance Contents

- KPI definitions and owner decisions.
- Screenshots or page inventory.
- Test values and reconciliation evidence.
- Known limitations, assumptions, and unresolved risks.
- Security and RLS/OLS review summary.
- Sign-off questions and acceptance status.

## Output Requirements

- Produce acceptance pack in business-readable language.
- Include en-US and de-DE notes for substantial artifacts.
