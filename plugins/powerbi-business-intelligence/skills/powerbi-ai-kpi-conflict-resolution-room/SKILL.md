# Power BI AI KPI Conflict Resolution Room

Use this skill before certifying disputed metrics.

## Conflict Areas

- Different numerator, denominator, date basis, grain, filters, exclusions, or currency handling.
- Department-specific local variants versus enterprise standard definition.
- Impact on reports, measures, targets, incentives, and decisions.
- Owner approval and change history.

## Output Requirements

- Produce conflict matrix, recommended certified definition, allowed variants, impacted artifacts, and decision log.
- Do not merge definitions when business semantics are genuinely different.
