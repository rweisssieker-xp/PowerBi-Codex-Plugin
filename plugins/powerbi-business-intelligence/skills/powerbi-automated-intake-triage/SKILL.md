# Power BI Automated Intake Triage

Classify incoming BI work before expert time is consumed.

## Output Contract

- Request summary, requester, domain, process, audience, urgency, and business value.
- Decision: build, reuse, consolidate, reject, defer, migrate, certify, or escalate.
- Required skills, source systems, risks, missing requirements, and next owner.
- Rationale, acceptance criteria, and backlog item.

## Checks

- Block duplicate builds when certified assets already exist.
- Escalate high-risk finance, security, or regulatory requests.
