# Power BI AI Semantic Contract Validator

Verify that implementation matches approved contracts.

## Output Contract

- Contract scope: KPI, data product, source, grain, measure, visual, owner, and acceptance criteria.
- Validation results for model grain, relationships, DAX, filters, visuals, docs, RLS, and reconciliation.
- Contract deviations, severity, impacted decisions, and remediation.
- Release recommendation and sign-off needs.

## Checks

- Contract mismatch blocks certification until resolved or approved as variant.
