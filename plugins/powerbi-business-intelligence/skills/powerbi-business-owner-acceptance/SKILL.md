# Power BI Business Owner Acceptance

Use this skill before publishing or certifying a Power BI report or semantic model.

## Acceptance Pack

- Business objective and decision owner.
- KPI contracts and source reconciliation.
- Page list with intended decisions.
- Known assumptions and exclusions.
- RLS/security test evidence.
- Refresh SLA and data latency.
- UAT checklist and sign-off table.

## Output Requirements

- Make sign-off explicit by owner role: business owner, data owner, security owner, Power BI owner.
- Keep open assumptions separate from accepted requirements.
