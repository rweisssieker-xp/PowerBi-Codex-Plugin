# Power BI ESG Compliance Source Pack

## Vendors

SAP Sustainability Control Tower, Microsoft Sustainability Manager, Workiva ESG, Sphera, Enablon, EcoVadis, Persefoni, Normative, Watershed.

## Source Objects

- Emission activity, emission factor, facility, energy meter, waste stream, water, supplier score, ESG disclosure, target, initiative, evidence.

## Power BI Patterns

- Scope 1/2/3 emissions.
- Energy intensity.
- Waste and water metrics.
- Supplier ESG risk.
- CSRD disclosure readiness.

## Output Requirements

- Include emission factor source, calculation method, reporting boundary, audit evidence, and restatement handling.
