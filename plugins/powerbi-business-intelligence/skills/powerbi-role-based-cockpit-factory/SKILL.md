# Power BI Role Based Cockpit Factory

Use this skill when the same data product needs role-specific decision views.

## Role Patterns

- CEO: growth, margin, cash, risk, strategic initiatives.
- CFO: P&L, working capital, cash, variance, controls.
- COO: delivery, production, quality, inventory, productivity.
- Sales Director: pipeline, order intake, margin, churn, forecast.
- Plant Manager: OEE, production plan, scrap, downtime, labor, safety.
- Supply Chain Manager: OTIF, inventory, supplier, demand, logistics.
- Controller: cost center, profit center, variance, allocations, close.
- Quality Manager: defects, claims, CAPA, audit, supplier quality.
- Maintenance Manager: asset availability, MTBF, MTTR, backlog, spare parts.

## Output Requirements

- Include decision owner, page set, KPI priority, drill paths, RLS scope, and action workflow.
