# Power BI Data Product Marketplace Generator

Generate a discoverable catalog of trusted BI assets.

## Output Contract

- Data product name, domain, owner, steward, source systems, refresh SLA, and certification status.
- KPI list, glossary terms, allowed use cases, RLS/OLS rules, and access request route.
- Quality score, lineage, known limitations, usage telemetry, and support channel.
- Marketplace page outline and `en-US`/`de-DE` documentation.

## Checks

- Separate certified products from sandbox assets.
- Include deprecation and replacement information.
