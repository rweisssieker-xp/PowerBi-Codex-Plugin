# Power BI Automated Report Review Rubric

Score report quality consistently before release, certification, or remediation.

## Score Areas

- Business value and decision clarity.
- KPI contracts and reconciliation.
- Semantic model design.
- DAX correctness and maintainability.
- Power Query folding and refresh reliability.
- UX, accessibility, drill paths, and actionability.
- RLS/OLS, sharing, sensitivity labels, and compliance.
- Performance, adoption, ownership, and documentation.

## Output Contract

- Scorecard, critical findings, remediation backlog, release recommendation, and evidence needs.
