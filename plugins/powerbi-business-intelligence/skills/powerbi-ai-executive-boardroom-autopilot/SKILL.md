# Power BI AI Executive Boardroom Autopilot

Use this skill for executive and board-level reporting.

## Boardroom Contents

- KPI movements, variance, root causes, risks, and opportunities.
- Decisions needed, accountable owners, action status, and financial impact.
- Confidence, data gaps, assumptions, and unresolved evidence.
- Changes since previous meeting and scenario options.

## Output Requirements

- Produce boardroom narrative, decision list, risk table, action table, and evidence notes.
- Separate facts from AI-generated interpretation.
