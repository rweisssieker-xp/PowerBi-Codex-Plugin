# Power BI Regulatory Standards

Use this skill when report outputs can become audit evidence, management reporting, regulatory disclosure, or controlled operational reporting.

## Coverage

- Finance: IFRS, HGB, US GAAP, SOX, internal controls, close evidence, segregation of duties.
- Sustainability: ESG, CSRD, energy, emissions, waste, supply chain due diligence.
- Quality: ISO 9001, IATF 16949, CAPA, complaints, audit findings, nonconformance.
- Life sciences: GMP, FDA, GxP, validation status, batch traceability, controlled changes.
- Trade and privacy: export control, sanctions, GDPR, data residency, external sharing.

## Output Requirements

- Identify regulated data, required evidence, owner sign-off, retention, lineage, and Quality Gate checks.
- Separate BI convenience reporting from official books, validated reports, and regulated submissions.
- Require compliance owner review when a report affects external reporting or controlled processes.
