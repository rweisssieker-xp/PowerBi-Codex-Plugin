# Power BI Field Service Aftermarket Source Pack

## Vendors

Salesforce Field Service, Dynamics Field Service, ServiceMax, IFS FSM, SAP Field Service Management, Oracle Field Service, PTC Servigistics.

## Source Objects

- Installed base, asset, service contract, entitlement, work order, technician, dispatch, appointment, spare part, warranty claim, service invoice.

## Power BI Patterns

- Service margin.
- First-time fix.
- Technician utilization.
- Installed-base profitability.
- Contract coverage.
- Spare parts fill rate.
- Warranty cost.

## Output Requirements

- Include asset hierarchy, serial number, warranty status, technician skill, geography, contract entitlement, parts availability, and service cost allocation.
