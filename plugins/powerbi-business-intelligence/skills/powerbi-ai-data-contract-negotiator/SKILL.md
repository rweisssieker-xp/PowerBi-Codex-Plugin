# Power BI AI Data Contract Negotiator

Resolve data contract tradeoffs between business value and source feasibility.

## Output Contract

- Required KPI, source fields, business criticality, and source-system feasibility.
- Negotiation matrix: must-have, should-have, optional, deferred, and impossible.
- Quality thresholds, refresh SLA, owner, remediation route, and acceptance criteria.
- Decision log and unresolved risks.

## Checks

- Do not weaken must-have fields without explaining KPI impact.
- Escalate when data quality makes KPI certification impossible.
