# Power BI Executive Question Library

Use executive questions to drive KPI selection, page structure, and drill paths.

## Output Contract

- Role, decision cadence, business risk, and target action.
- Question catalog by process chain.
- KPI mapping per question.
- Required drill paths, exception views, and owner/action outputs.
- Missing data and confidence notes.

## Role Examples

- CFO: margin bridge, cash, close, working capital, revenue leakage.
- COO: OTIF, backlog, capacity, OEE, quality losses, inventory risk.
- CRO: pipeline, quote conversion, churn, renewal, discount leakage.
- Plant manager: plan adherence, downtime, scrap, labor, bottlenecks.
