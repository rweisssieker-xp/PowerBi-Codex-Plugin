# Power BI Data Lineage Risk Scorer

Score how risky a KPI or artifact is before it becomes trusted.

## Score Dimensions

- Number and criticality of sources.
- Manual steps and spreadsheets.
- Transformation complexity.
- Owner clarity.
- Refresh dependency.
- KPI definition maturity.
- RLS/PII sensitivity.
- Downstream reports and decisions.

## Output Contract

- Risk score, risk drivers, impacted assets, and mitigation backlog.
- Lineage gaps, missing owners, untested transformations, and evidence needs.
- Certification impact and release recommendation.
