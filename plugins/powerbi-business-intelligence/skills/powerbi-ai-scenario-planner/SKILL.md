# Power BI AI Scenario Planner

Use this skill when planning and simulation need AI-generated scenario sets.

## Scenario Pattern

- Define baseline and controlled variables.
- Generate plausible scenarios with assumptions.
- Estimate impact and sensitivity.
- Identify required data and model formulas.
- Recommend scenario visual and decision owner.

## Output Requirements

- Include assumptions, variables, impact measure, confidence, and owner.
- Separate exploratory scenarios from approved planning values.
