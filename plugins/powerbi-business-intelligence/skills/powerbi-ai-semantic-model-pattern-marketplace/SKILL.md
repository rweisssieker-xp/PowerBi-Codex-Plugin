# Power BI AI Semantic Model Pattern Marketplace

Use this skill to reuse certified modeling patterns.

## Pattern Contents

- Business process, grain, facts, dimensions, bridges, role-playing dates, and relationships.
- Required source fields, keys, history, and data quality assumptions.
- Standard measures, calculation patterns, RLS/OLS notes, and performance considerations.
- Known anti-patterns and validation tests.

## Output Requirements

- Recommend a pattern, explain fit/gaps, and list required adaptations.
- Mark unsupported relationship or source assumptions as blockers.
