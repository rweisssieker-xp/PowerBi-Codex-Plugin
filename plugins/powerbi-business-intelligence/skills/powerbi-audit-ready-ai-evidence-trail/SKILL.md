# Power BI Audit Ready AI Evidence Trail

Make AI-generated BI work reviewable and defensible.

## Output Contract

- AI output, prompt context, user intent, model assumptions, and prohibited-data check.
- Sources, metadata evidence, KPI contracts, tests, reconciliation results, and confidence score.
- Human reviewer, approval state, change history, and downstream impact.
- Residual risk and required follow-up.

## Checks

- No unsupported recommendations.
- No hidden assumptions.
- No production use without owner approval where governance requires it.
