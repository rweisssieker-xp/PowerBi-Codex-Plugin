# Power BI Automated Requirement Interview

Drive requirements discovery with structured follow-up questions.

## Output Contract

- Known facts, unknowns, assumptions, and risk blockers.
- Question set by KPI, process, source, grain, date basis, owner, RLS, refresh, visuals, validation, and acceptance.
- Completed requirement summary and remaining open decisions.
- Backlog-ready implementation brief.

## Checks

- Ask only questions needed to unblock design.
- Convert answers into explicit contracts.
