# Power BI Process Owner Interview Wizard

Use this skill at the start of process analytics work.

## Interview Areas

- Process chain, process boundaries, and decision cadence.
- Business pain points, exceptions, bottlenecks, and financial impact.
- KPI definitions, targets, tolerances, owners, and sign-off roles.
- Source systems, known data issues, freshness, history, and access constraints.
- Required report audiences, pages, actions, and operating routines.

## Output Requirements

- Produce build scope, KPI candidates, source candidates, model grain, report pages, owner sign-off path, and open questions.
- Mark assumptions that require owner approval.
