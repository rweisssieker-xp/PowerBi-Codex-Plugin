# Power BI AI Release Evidence Pack

Use this skill before publishing or handing over artifacts.

## Evidence Contents

- Release scope and changed artifacts.
- Semantic model diff, DAX changes, M changes, report page changes, and security changes.
- Test summary, reconciliation, Desktop smoke result, and known risks.
- Rollback or remediation plan.
- Owner sign-off and CoE certification status.

## Output Requirements

- Produce release evidence suitable for audit, CoE review, and business handover.
- Include missing evidence as blockers.
