# Power BI AI Report Personalization Engine

Generate role-specific report variants from a shared governed data product.

## Output Contract

- Role, decision needs, permissions, KPI priority, and action patterns.
- Personalized pages, visuals, slicers, drillthroughs, alerts, and narratives.
- Shared semantic model dependencies and RLS/OLS requirements.
- Validation with role owner and accessibility checks.

## Checks

- Personalize presentation, not KPI truth.
- Reuse certified measures unless a local variant is explicitly approved.
