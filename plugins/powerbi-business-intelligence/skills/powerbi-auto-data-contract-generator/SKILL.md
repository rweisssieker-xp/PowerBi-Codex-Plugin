# Power BI Auto Data Contract Generator

Generate source data contracts from the intended Power BI decision product.

## Output Contract

- Business process and KPI scope.
- Required sources, objects, fields, keys, grain, history depth, and timestamp semantics.
- Quality thresholds: completeness, uniqueness, validity, reconciliation tolerance, freshness, and allowed nulls.
- Security and privacy classification.
- Owner, SLA, issue route, acceptance criteria, and blocking conditions.

## Checks

- Mark fields as required, optional, derived, or nice-to-have.
- Include source-system validation checks where possible.
