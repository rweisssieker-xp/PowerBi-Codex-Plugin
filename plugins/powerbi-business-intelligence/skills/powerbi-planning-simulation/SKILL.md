# Power BI Planning Simulation

Use this skill when the business needs decisions and scenarios, not only historical reporting.

## Patterns

- Price/volume/mix simulation.
- Demand forecast and inventory what-if.
- Capacity planning and constraint simulation.
- Working capital scenario: DSO, DPO, inventory days.
- Budget and forecast variance.
- Order backlog and delivery promise simulation.
- Supplier delay and material shortage impact.

## Architecture Options

- Power BI what-if parameters for lightweight scenarios.
- Writeback through Power Apps, Dataverse, Fabric, SQL, or planning tools.
- External planning systems such as SAP IBP, Anaplan, Kinaxis, Oracle Planning, Excel-controlled input.

## Output Requirements

- Include scenario grain, input ownership, writeback target, security, audit trail, calculation logic, and validation.
- Flag when Power BI is only the visualization layer and planning ownership sits elsewhere.
