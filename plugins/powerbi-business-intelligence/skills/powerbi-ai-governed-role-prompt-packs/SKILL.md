# Power BI AI Governed Role Prompt Packs

Use this skill to standardize safe role-based AI usage.

## Prompt Pack Roles

- Process Owner and Process Manager.
- Data Analyst and BI Designer.
- Data Steward and Source Owner.
- CoE Reviewer and Governance Owner.
- Executive and Boardroom user.
- IT, Security, and Fabric administrator.

## Output Requirements

- Include approved prompts, prohibited prompts, required evidence, escalation path, and output templates.
- Align prompts with hallucination guard, data policy, and certification rules.
