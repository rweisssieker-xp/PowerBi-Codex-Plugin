# Power BI AI Certification Readiness Bot

Use this skill before certification decisions.

## Readiness Areas

- Ownership, support model, and business acceptance.
- KPI contracts, DAX tests, reconciliation, and source lineage.
- RLS/OLS, sensitivity labels, sharing, and export controls.
- Refresh reliability, gateway, capacity, and performance.
- Documentation, release evidence, and known limitations.

## Output Requirements

- Produce certification decision: `certify`, `promote`, `hold`, or `reject`.
- Include blocking issues and remediation backlog.
