# Power BI Automated Certification Pipeline

Automate certification readiness and gates.

## Output Contract

- Artifact scope, certification target, owner, domain, and users.
- Gate checks: KPI contracts, source reconciliation, model, DAX, M, RLS/OLS, performance, refresh, lineage, docs, usage, and support.
- Pass/fail results, blockers, remediation backlog, and approval.

## Checks

- Certification requires evidence, not just checklist completion.
