# Vendor Process Object Catalog

Object names are candidate families. Verify in the target tenant or warehouse.

## SAP

- Lead2Order: business partner, lead, opportunity, quotation, sales order, pricing condition, partner function.
- Order2Cash: sales order, schedule line, delivery, goods issue, billing, AR open item, payment, dispute.
- Procure2Pay: purchase requisition, purchase order, schedule line, confirmation, goods receipt, invoice receipt, vendor item.
- Plan2Produce: material, BOM, routing, work center, planned order, production order, confirmation, goods movement, inspection lot.
- Record2Report: universal journal, GL account, cost center, profit center, internal order, WBS, plan, allocation.
- Maintain2Operate: equipment, functional location, notification, maintenance order, task list, measurement document.

## Microsoft Dynamics

- Lead2Order: lead, account, contact, opportunity, quote, order, price list, product.
- Order2Cash: sales order, sales line, shipment, invoice, customer ledger, payment, collection.
- Procure2Pay: purchase requisition, purchase order, receipt, vendor invoice, vendor transaction.
- Plan2Produce: released product, BOM, route, production order, operation, inventory transaction.
- Record2Report: main account, financial dimension, ledger journal, voucher, budget, fixed asset.
- Service: case, work order, asset, agreement, booking, service task.

## Salesforce

- Lead2Order: lead, account, contact, opportunity, quote, quote line, contract, order.
- Service: case, entitlement, milestone, work order, asset, service appointment.
- Revenue: subscription, amendment, invoice, usage, revenue schedule depending on Revenue Cloud/CPQ.

## Oracle

- Lead2Order/CX: lead, account, opportunity, quote, order.
- Order2Cash: order header/line, shipment, invoice, receivable, receipt, dispute.
- Procure2Pay: requisition, purchase order, receipt, AP invoice, payment.
- Record2Report: journal, ledger, chart of accounts, cost center, project, budget, asset.
- HCM: worker, assignment, absence, payroll aggregate, learning.

## Infor, IFS, Epicor, QAD

- Manufacturing: item, BOM, routing, work center, production order, operation, labor, material issue, receipt, scrap.
- Supply chain: purchase order, supplier, receipt, inventory transaction, warehouse order, shipment.
- Projects/service: project, work order, asset, service contract, field service task.
- Finance: ledger, account, cost center, voucher, invoice, payment.

## Workday, SuccessFactors, HR Systems

- Worker, position, organization, job, compensation aggregate, absence, learning, recruiting stage, performance.
- Treat payroll, health, diversity, and performance data as sensitive by default.

## ServiceNow, Jira, Zendesk, HubSpot

- ServiceNow: incident, change, problem, request, task, CMDB CI, SLA, assignment group.
- Jira: issue, sprint, project, component, status transition, assignee, release.
- Zendesk: ticket, requester, organization, group, SLA, satisfaction.
- HubSpot: contact, company, deal, ticket, engagement.
