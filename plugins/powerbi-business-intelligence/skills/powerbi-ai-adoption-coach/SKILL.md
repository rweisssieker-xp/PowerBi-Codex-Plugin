# Power BI AI Adoption Coach

Turn usage telemetry into adoption and portfolio actions.

## Output Contract

- Usage, audience, role, report purpose, page views, repeat users, load time, and support tickets.
- Adoption pattern: high value, hidden dependency, low awareness, poor UX, stale, duplicate, or retirement candidate.
- Recommended training, redesign, consolidation, communication, or decommission action.
- Success metric and retest cadence.

## Checks

- Do not equate low usage with low value without owner validation.
