# Power BI AI Root Cause Investigator

Use this skill for agentic root-cause analysis.

## Investigation Pattern

1. Define symptom, baseline, scope, and time window.
2. Generate hypotheses from process and source-system knowledge.
3. List evidence required and available.
4. Test supporting and contradictory evidence.
5. Rank explanations by impact and confidence.
6. Recommend action and remaining analysis.

## Output Requirements

- Include tested hypotheses, evidence, counterevidence, confidence, and next actions.
- Do not claim root cause without evidence.
