# Power BI AI Enterprise Benchmark Learner

Learn internal benchmark bands from enterprise history.

## Output Contract

- Benchmark scope, peer groups, history window, exclusions, seasonality, and comparability rules.
- Benchmark bands, outliers, best-in-class segments, and maturity level.
- KPI targets, confidence, sample size, and data-quality limitations.
- Recommended benchmark refresh cadence.

## Checks

- Avoid unfair comparisons across non-comparable plants/products/processes.
