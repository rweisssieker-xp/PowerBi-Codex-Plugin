# Power BI Business Glossary Generator

Use this skill to make reports understandable and governable.

## Glossary Entries

- Term and synonyms.
- Business definition.
- Process and domain.
- Source system and source field.
- DAX measure or semantic model field.
- Owner and steward.
- Security/sensitivity.
- Related reports and KPIs.
- Approved translation.

## Output Requirements

- Mark draft versus approved terms.
- Detect synonym conflicts and duplicate definitions.
