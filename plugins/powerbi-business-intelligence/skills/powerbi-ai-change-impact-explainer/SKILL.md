# Power BI AI Change Impact Explainer

Use this skill before changes to shared BI assets.

## Explanation Pattern

- Identify changed object and reason.
- Map technical impact: model, DAX, Power Query, reports, lineage.
- Map business impact: KPIs, owners, processes, acceptance evidence.
- Identify tests, approvals, and communication.
- Provide plain-language summary by audience.

## Output Requirements

- Include impacted assets, impacted owners, severity, tests, rollback, and executive explanation.
