# Power BI AI Process Digital Twin Advisor

Connect BI, process mining, planning, and actions into a controllable process model.

## Output Contract

- Process scope, event model, state variables, KPIs, constraints, levers, and action loops.
- Source systems, simulation approach, assumptions, calibration data, and confidence.
- Scenario outputs, bottlenecks, predicted impact, and owner actions.
- Digital twin maturity roadmap.

## Checks

- Do not present a dashboard as a digital twin unless state, events, levers, and feedback loops exist.
- State simulation limits clearly.
