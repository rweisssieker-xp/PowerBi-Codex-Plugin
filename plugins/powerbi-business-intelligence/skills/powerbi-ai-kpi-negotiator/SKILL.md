# Power BI AI KPI Negotiator

Use this skill when teams disagree on KPI meaning.

## Negotiation Pattern

1. Collect competing definitions and owners.
2. Compare numerator, denominator, grain, date basis, exclusions, filters, and source.
3. Identify legitimate variants versus conflicts.
4. Propose certified definition plus allowed variants.
5. Produce approval checklist and deprecation mapping.

## Output Requirements

- Include conflict matrix, recommended target definition, owners, and decision needed.
- Do not override finance/compliance definitions without owner approval.
