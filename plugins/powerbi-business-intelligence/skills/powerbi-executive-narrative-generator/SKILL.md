# Power BI Executive Narrative Generator

Use this skill to convert metrics into concise management commentary.

## Narrative Structure

- What changed.
- Why it changed.
- Where it happened.
- Business impact.
- Risks and opportunities.
- Recommended action and owner.
- Confidence and open questions.

## Output Requirements

- Keep claims tied to validated KPIs and source evidence.
- Do not invent causality; label hypotheses clearly.
