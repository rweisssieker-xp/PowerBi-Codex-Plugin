# Power BI Automated Executive Briefing

Generate executive briefings from governed BI evidence.

## Output Contract

- Audience, meeting cadence, period, decision agenda, and KPI scope.
- KPI movements, variance drivers, root causes, risks, scenarios, decisions needed, and action follow-up.
- Evidence, confidence, open data gaps, and owner list.
- `en-US` and `de-DE` briefing when substantial.

## Checks

- Separate fact, interpretation, scenario, and recommendation.
