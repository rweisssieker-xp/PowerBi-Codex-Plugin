# Power BI AI Ticket Resolver

Use this skill for operational Power BI support.

## Ticket Types

- Refresh failed.
- Wrong number or KPI mismatch.
- Slow report.
- Access/RLS issue.
- Missing data.
- Broken visual or field.
- Deployment or gateway failure.

## Output Requirements

- Include classification, likely cause, evidence needed, owner, resolution steps, and prevention.
- Escalate security and regulated-report issues to the correct owner.
