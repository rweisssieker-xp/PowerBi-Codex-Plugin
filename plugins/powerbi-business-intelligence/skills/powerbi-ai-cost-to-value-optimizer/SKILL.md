# Power BI AI Cost-to-Value Optimizer

Use this skill for FinOps and portfolio value management.

## Optimization Areas

- Capacity usage, refresh cost, model size, query load, and storage mode.
- Usage, audience, decision value, process impact, and certification status.
- Import versus DirectQuery versus Direct Lake tradeoffs.
- Expensive low-value artifacts and high-value under-supported artifacts.

## Output Requirements

- Produce cost-to-value score, optimization actions, expected savings, risk, and owner.
- Do not optimize cost at the expense of critical process or compliance reporting.
