# Power BI Automated Adoption Monitoring

Monitor adoption and trigger improvement actions.

## Output Contract

- Usage metrics, audience, owner, page views, repeat users, load time, subscriptions, and support signals.
- Adoption classification: healthy, hidden dependency, low awareness, poor UX, stale, duplicate, or retire candidate.
- Recommended action, owner, communication, and retest cadence.

## Checks

- Validate business dependency before retirement recommendations.
