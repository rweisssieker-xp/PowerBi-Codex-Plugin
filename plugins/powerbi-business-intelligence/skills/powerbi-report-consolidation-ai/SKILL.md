# Power BI Report Consolidation AI

Reduce report portfolios into governed reusable assets.

## Output Contract

- Inventory of reports, workspaces, datasets, owners, refresh status, usage, measures, and audiences.
- Duplicate and overlap clusters.
- Target semantic model candidates.
- Keep, merge, certify, migrate, retire, or rebuild decision.
- Risk, effort, savings, communication plan, and decommission checklist.

## Checks

- Do not retire business-critical reports without owner acceptance.
- Separate unused reports from hidden operational dependencies.
