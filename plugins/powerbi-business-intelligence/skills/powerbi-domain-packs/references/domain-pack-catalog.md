# Domain Pack Catalog

## Customer Churn

Sources: CRM, contracts, invoices, usage telemetry, support cases.
Facts: customer-period, contract, invoice, usage event, support event.
KPIs: churn rate, retained revenue, lost ARR/MRR, NRR, GRR, usage decline, risk score.
Pages: overview, cohort retention, churn drivers, at-risk customers, customer drillthrough.

## Material Churn

Sources: ERP material master, stock snapshots, movements, purchase orders, forecast.
Facts: material-location-period, goods movement, purchase order line, demand forecast.
KPIs: slow-moving value, obsolete stock, inventory turns, stockout count, scrap, forecast bias.
Pages: overview, ABC/XYZ, stock aging, supplier risk, material exceptions.

## FiCO

Sources: GL line items, controlling documents, plan data, open items, master data.
Facts: journal line, plan line, balance snapshot, invoice, payment.
KPIs: actuals, plan, variance, margin, DSO, DPO, working capital, open items.
Pages: executive finance, P&L, cost center, profit center, variance bridge, open item detail.

## Sales

Sources: CRM opportunities, orders, invoices, product/customer master.
Facts: opportunity, order line, invoice line, account-period.
KPIs: pipeline, weighted pipeline, win rate, bookings, revenue, margin, forecast accuracy.
Pages: sales overview, funnel, pipeline aging, territory, account drillthrough.

## Procurement

Sources: purchase requisitions, purchase orders, receipts, invoices, supplier master.
Facts: PO line, receipt, invoice, supplier-period.
KPIs: spend, savings, maverick buying, on-time delivery, price variance, supplier risk.
Pages: spend overview, category analysis, supplier scorecard, compliance, PO exceptions.

## Service Operations

Sources: tickets, work orders, assets, SLA events, team calendars.
Facts: ticket, work order, SLA event, backlog snapshot.
KPIs: backlog, SLA compliance, MTTA, MTTR, reopen rate, throughput, workload.
Pages: service overview, SLA risk, backlog aging, root causes, team workload.

## HR Workforce

Sources: HRIS, recruiting, absence, learning, payroll aggregates.
Facts: employee snapshot, recruiting stage, absence, training record.
KPIs: headcount, attrition, vacancy, time-to-hire, absence rate, training completion.
Pages: workforce overview, attrition, recruiting funnel, absence, compliance.

## Risk and Compliance

Sources: controls, audit findings, incidents, policy exceptions, assessments.
Facts: finding, control test, incident, exception.
KPIs: open findings, overdue actions, residual risk, control pass rate, incident severity.
Pages: risk overview, heatmap, control effectiveness, overdue actions, audit detail.
