# Power BI Value Realization Tracker

Measure whether BI outputs changed decisions, actions, cost, and expert workload.

## Output Contract

- Baseline: expert hours, report count, failures, decision latency, manual effort, and cost.
- Target benefits: consolidation, automation, certified models, faster close, lower leakage, reduced rework.
- Value measures, owner, cadence, and evidence source.
- Realized value, confidence, attribution limits, and next actions.

## Checks

- Do not claim savings without baseline and evidence.
- Separate adoption metrics from financial value.
