# Power BI AI Business Question Generator

Generate useful business questions from model and process context.

## Output Contract

- Role, decision cadence, process scope, and source coverage.
- Question catalog grouped by executive, tactical, operational, exception, and root-cause questions.
- KPI, model object, visual, and drillthrough mapping per question.
- Missing-data and confidence notes.

## Checks

- Prioritize questions that can drive action.
- Flag questions that cannot be answered with current data.
