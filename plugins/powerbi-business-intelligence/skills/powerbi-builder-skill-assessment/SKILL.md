# Power BI Builder Skill Assessment

Assess who should build what and what the plugin can standardize.

## Output Contract

- Builder role inventory and capability score.
- Task classification: self-service, guided self-service, CoE-owned, IT-owned, expert-only, or plugin-automated.
- Risk by domain: DAX, modelling, source access, RLS, performance, governance, documentation.
- Training plan, guardrails, approval thresholds, and expert-reduction roadmap.

## Checks

- Do not move high-risk finance/security reports into unmanaged self-service.
- Tie skill gaps to concrete delivery artifacts.
