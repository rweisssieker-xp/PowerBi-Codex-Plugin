# Power BI Automated Capacity Optimization

Automate capacity and refresh optimization recommendations.

## Output Contract

- Capacity, workspace, dataset, refresh, query, model size, storage, and usage signals.
- Bottleneck classification and recommended optimization.
- Impact estimate, risk, test plan, and approval need.
- Monitoring cadence after change.

## Checks

- Do not reduce cost at the expense of agreed SLAs.
