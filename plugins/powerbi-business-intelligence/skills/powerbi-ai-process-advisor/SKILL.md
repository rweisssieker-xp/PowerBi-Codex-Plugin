# Power BI AI Process Advisor

Use this skill after validated process analysis or root-cause findings.

## Advice Pattern

- Identify process symptom and validated drivers.
- Separate policy, system, data, capacity, supplier, customer, and execution issues.
- Recommend process actions with owner and expected impact.
- Define monitoring KPI and feedback loop.

## Output Requirements

- Include evidence, recommendation, owner, impact, effort, risk, and follow-up metric.
- Label unvalidated advice as hypothesis.
