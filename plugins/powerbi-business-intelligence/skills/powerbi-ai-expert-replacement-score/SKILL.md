# Power BI AI Expert Replacement Score

Score the right execution path for BI work.

## Output Contract

- Task type, business criticality, data sensitivity, source complexity, KPI maturity, model risk, DAX complexity, and delivery urgency.
- Execution recommendation: AI/plugin, self-service, guided self-service, CoE, IT, senior expert, or hybrid.
- Automation potential, risk, required reviews, and expected expert-hour savings.
- Escalation triggers and acceptance criteria.

## Checks

- Do not route high-risk finance/security artifacts to unmanaged self-service.
- Tie score to evidence, not optimism.
