# Power BI AI Model Governance

Use this skill for predictive or generative AI in BI workflows.

## Governance Requirements

- Model purpose, owner, training data, target variable.
- Validation metric and threshold.
- Bias/fairness and segment performance.
- Drift monitoring and retraining trigger.
- Explainability and human review.
- Approval, versioning, audit trail, and retirement.

## Output Requirements

- Produce a model card and governance checklist.
- Flag high-impact decisions for human-in-the-loop approval.
