# Power BI AI Self Service Copilot Guard

Guide self-service builders while blocking risky choices.

## Output Contract

- Builder role, data sensitivity, intended audience, source systems, and artifact type.
- Allowed actions, warning actions, blocked actions, and escalation route.
- Guidance for modelling, DAX, visuals, RLS, refresh, sharing, export, and documentation.
- Review checklist and handoff path to CoE or expert.

## Checks

- Block unmanaged sensitive data sharing.
- Escalate certified KPI or finance/security changes.
