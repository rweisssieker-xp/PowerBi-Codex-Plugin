# Power BI Lineage and Impact Analyzer

Use this skill before changing shared Power BI assets.

## Impact Areas

- Source field or table changes.
- Power Query transformations.
- Semantic model relationships and hidden columns.
- DAX measures and calculation groups.
- Report pages, visuals, filters, bookmarks, drillthrough.
- RLS/OLS roles and sensitivity labels.
- Refresh schedule, gateway, deployment pipeline.
- Downstream reports and business owners.

## Output Requirements

- Produce impacted assets, owners, severity, test scope, rollout plan, and rollback notes.
- Flag certified datasets and executive/finance reports as high scrutiny.
