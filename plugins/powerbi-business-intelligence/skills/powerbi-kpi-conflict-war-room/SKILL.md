# Power BI KPI Conflict War Room

Resolve metric conflicts before building or certifying reports.

## Output Contract

- Conflicting KPI names, synonyms, owners, use cases, and impacted reports.
- Competing definitions with numerator, denominator, date basis, exclusions, grain, currency/unit logic, and reconciliation source.
- Recommended certified definition and allowed local variants.
- Decision log, unresolved issues, approval owners, and migration impact.

## Checks

- Identify CFO, Controlling, Sales, and Operations definition conflicts explicitly.
- Classify variants as certified, local, deprecated, or forbidden.
