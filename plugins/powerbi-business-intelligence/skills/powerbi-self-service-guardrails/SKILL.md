# Power BI Self-Service Guardrails

Use this skill to scale Power BI safely without routing every report through senior experts.

## Guardrail Areas

- Data: certified datasets only, approved dimensions, no local sensitive extracts unless approved.
- Measures: certified measures preferred; custom measures require owner and validation.
- Visuals: standard page patterns, clear slicers, no misleading charts, no hidden filters without documentation.
- Security: RLS inherited from datasets, no broad export unless permitted, sensitivity labels required.
- Delivery: review gates for finance, HR, external sharing, executive reporting, and regulated data.

## Output Requirements

- Produce rules as allowed, restricted, and prohibited actions.
- Include escalation path and review triggers.
