# Power BI Closed Loop Action ROI

Track whether insights became actions and whether actions solved the problem.

## Output Contract

- Insight, exception KPI, recommended action, owner, due date, and workflow target.
- Baseline KPI, expected impact, realized impact, cost, and confidence.
- Closure status, effectiveness check, retest date, and recurrence.
- ROI summary by process, owner, report, and data product.

## Checks

- Distinguish task closure from KPI improvement.
- Require evidence before claiming realized value.
