# Power BI Treasury Cash Source Pack

## Vendors

Kyriba, SAP Treasury, Serrala, TIS, Coupa Treasury, Oracle Treasury, banking APIs, EBICS, SWIFT, host-to-host bank connections.

## Source Objects

- Bank account, bank statement, cash position, cash forecast, payment run, liquidity item, treasury deal, FX exposure, hedge, bank fee.

## Power BI Patterns

- Cash position and forecast.
- Working capital and liquidity.
- Payment status and exceptions.
- FX exposure and hedging.
- Bank fee analysis.

## Output Requirements

- Include bank/account security, currency conversion, value date versus posting date, legal entity, and bank reconciliation evidence.
