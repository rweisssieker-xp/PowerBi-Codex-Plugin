# Power BI AI Cross Process Dependency Mapper

Map cross-process dependencies that explain business outcomes.

## Output Contract

- Source process, target process, dependency type, shared objects, timing, and owner.
- KPI dependency graph with upstream drivers and downstream effects.
- Source systems, master data keys, event links, and validation evidence.
- Risk, action owner, and dashboard/drillthrough recommendation.

## Checks

- Do not join process data without proven keys and timing semantics.
