# Power BI Migration Cost Estimator

Estimate migration waves, cost, risk, and savings.

## Output Contract

- Source inventory, complexity drivers, dependencies, usage, business criticality, and target pattern.
- Effort estimate by discovery, data access, modelling, DAX, visuals, testing, security, deployment, docs, and change management.
- Migration sequence, consolidation opportunity, decommission savings, and residual risk.
- Acceptance and rollback plan.

## Checks

- Do not estimate purely by report count.
- Include semantic model reuse and KPI conflict risk.
