# Business Problem Patterns

## Customer Churn

Grain: customer-period, subscription-period, contract, order, usage event, support case.
Measures: active customers, churned customers, churn rate, retained revenue, lost revenue, NRR/GRR, usage decline, risk score, cohort retention.
Visuals: KPI cards, cohort matrix, retention curve, churn reasons, risk segment scatter, customer table with next-best-action.

## Material Churn and Inventory Risk

Grain: material-location-period, movement, purchase order, stock snapshot.
Measures: inventory turns, obsolete stock, slow moving material, stockout count, supplier lead time, scrap, demand volatility, forecast bias.
Visuals: ABC/XYZ matrix, stock aging, movement waterfall, supplier risk heatmap, material exception table.

## FiCO and Finance

Grain: journal line, cost-center-period, profit-center-period, invoice, payment, plan line.
Measures: actuals, plan, variance, margin, working capital, DSO, DPO, cash conversion, accruals, open items.
Visuals: P&L matrix, variance bridge, cost center drilldown, working-capital trend, overdue receivables, management cockpit.

## Sales and Pipeline

Grain: opportunity, account-period, order line, invoice line.
Measures: pipeline, weighted pipeline, win rate, sales cycle, conversion, bookings, revenue, margin, forecast accuracy.
Visuals: funnel, pipeline aging, cohort conversion, territory map, account ranking.

## Operations and Service

Grain: ticket, work order, event, asset-period, queue snapshot.
Measures: backlog, throughput, SLA compliance, cycle time, first-time-right, utilization, rework, defect rate.
Visuals: flow trend, queue aging, root cause Pareto, SLA risk table, team workload.
