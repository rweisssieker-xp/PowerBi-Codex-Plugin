# Power BI Requirement to Model Autopilot

Convert business language into an implementation-ready Power BI design.

## Output Contract

- Business objective, decision owner, cadence, process chain, and audience.
- KPI tree with outcome, driver, exception, and action KPIs.
- Candidate sources, source objects, keys, grain, date roles, and blockers.
- Semantic model: facts, dimensions, relationships, RLS, refresh mode, and certified dataset candidate.
- Report pages, visuals, drillthroughs, validation plan, backlog, and `en-US`/`de-DE` documentation.

## Checks

- Do not create visuals before grain and KPI contracts are explicit.
- Flag missing source ownership, missing history, unclear status definitions, and manual Excel dependencies.
