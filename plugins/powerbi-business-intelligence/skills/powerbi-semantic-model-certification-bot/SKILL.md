# Power BI Semantic Model Certification Bot

Assess whether a model can become a certified reusable enterprise asset.

## Output Contract

- Certification score and blocker list.
- Ownership, domain, SLA, refresh, gateway, endorsement, and support model.
- KPI contracts and approved measure catalog.
- DAX, relationship, RLS/OLS, performance, lineage, and documentation checks.
- Required remediation backlog and sign-off evidence.

## Certification States

- Candidate.
- Blocked.
- Certifiable after remediation.
- Certified.
- Deprecated.
