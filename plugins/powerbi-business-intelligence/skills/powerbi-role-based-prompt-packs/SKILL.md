# Power BI Role Based Prompt Packs

Create safe, reusable prompts for enterprise BI roles.

## Output Contract

- Role, permissions, allowed domains, source systems, and prohibited data.
- Prompt pack for discovery, KPI design, root cause, report review, quality gate, and documentation.
- Expected output format and required evidence.
- Guardrails for PII, financial sensitivity, hallucination checks, and owner approval.

## Roles

CEO, CFO, COO, CRO, controller, sales ops, supply chain, procurement, quality, plant, maintenance, service, HR, IT, data steward, CoE.
