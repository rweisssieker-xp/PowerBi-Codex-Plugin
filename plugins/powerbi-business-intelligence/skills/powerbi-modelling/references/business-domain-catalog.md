# Business Domain Catalog

## Core Dimensions

Date, organization, company code, cost center, profit center, customer, supplier, product, material, asset, employee, geography, channel, scenario, currency, status, owner, source system.

## Common Fact Tables

- Finance: journal line, balance snapshot, plan, invoice, payment, open item.
- Sales: opportunity, quote, order, invoice line, shipment, customer-period.
- Supply chain: material movement, inventory snapshot, purchase order line, supplier delivery, demand forecast.
- Service: case, work order, SLA event, backlog snapshot, asset service history.
- HR: employee snapshot, absence, recruiting stage, learning record, payroll aggregate.
- Risk/compliance: control test, audit finding, incident, policy exception.

## Common Measures

Amount, quantity, count, active entities, new entities, closed entities, backlog, conversion rate, retention, churn, variance, margin, aging, cycle time, SLA compliance, forecast accuracy, coverage, utilization.
