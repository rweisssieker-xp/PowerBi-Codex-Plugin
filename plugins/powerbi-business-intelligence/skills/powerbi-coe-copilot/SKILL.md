# Power BI Center of Excellence Copilot

Use this skill when the organization wants an operating model, not a single report.

## CoE Capabilities

- Intake routing and triage.
- Certified dataset and KPI governance.
- Portfolio rationalization and retirement.
- Self-service guardrails.
- Expert review and remediation.
- Fabric architecture standards.
- Migration factory and delivery factory.
- Cost and capacity optimization.
- Business owner acceptance.
- Enablement, templates, and recurring governance cadence.

## Output Requirements

- Produce standards, operating cadence, roles, review gates, scorecards, and adoption roadmap.
- Focus on reducing repeated expert involvement while preserving security and business correctness.
