# Power BI AI Data Quality to Business Impact

Use this skill to make data quality actionable.

## Impact Areas

- Missing keys, duplicate master data, invalid dates, orphan facts, stale records, and wrong units.
- KPI distortion, report trust risk, process impact, and financial exposure.
- Affected Process Owners, Data Stewards, source systems, and remediation owners.
- Certification blockers and retest criteria.

## Output Requirements

- Produce DQ issue, affected KPIs, estimated impact, owner, remediation, and retest plan.
- Distinguish measured impact from estimated impact.
