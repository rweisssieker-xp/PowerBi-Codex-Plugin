# Power BI QM QMS EHS Source Pack

## Vendors and Systems

SAP QM, SAP EHS, ETQ Reliance, MasterControl, TrackWise, Intelex, Sphera EHS, Enablon, Veeva Quality, LabWare LIMS, STARLIMS, Sparta, AssurX.

## Source Objects

- Quality notification, inspection lot, defect, nonconformance, CAPA, audit, complaint, deviation, change control, batch release, lab result, EHS incident, safety observation.

## Power BI Patterns

- Cost of poor quality.
- CAPA aging and effectiveness.
- Complaint and warranty trends.
- Supplier quality.
- Audit findings and overdue actions.
- Inspection yield and defect Pareto.
- EHS incidents and safety performance.

## Output Requirements

- Include severity, product/material, batch/lot, supplier, plant, root cause, action owner, due date, effectiveness check, regulatory impact, and evidence status.
- Treat patient, employee, safety, and regulated quality data as sensitive by default.
