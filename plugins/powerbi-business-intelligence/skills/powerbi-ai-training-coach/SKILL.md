# Power BI AI Training Coach

Use this skill for role-specific enablement.

## Coaching Pattern

- Identify role, skill level, domain, and tool access.
- Select relevant reports, KPIs, governance rules, and exercises.
- Provide guided tasks and feedback.
- Test understanding with role-specific scenarios.

## Output Requirements

- Include learning path, exercises, checklist, and assessment.
- Keep coaching aligned to approved glossary, KPIs, and CoE standards.
