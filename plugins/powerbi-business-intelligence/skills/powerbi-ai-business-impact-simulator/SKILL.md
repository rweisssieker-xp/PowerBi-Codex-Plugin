# Power BI AI Business Impact Simulator

Estimate business impact from BI findings and scenarios.

## Output Contract

- Scenario/action, baseline, affected KPIs, assumptions, constraints, and time horizon.
- Impact model: revenue, margin, cash, inventory, cost, service, quality, risk, or productivity.
- Sensitivity ranges, confidence, evidence, counterfactual, and risks.
- Recommended decision and retest plan.

## Checks

- Separate simulation from forecast and actuals.
- State assumptions that dominate the result.
