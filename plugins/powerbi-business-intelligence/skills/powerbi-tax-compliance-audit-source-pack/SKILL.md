# Power BI Tax Compliance Audit Source Pack

## Vendors

Vertex, Avalara, Thomson Reuters ONESOURCE, SAP GRC, Oracle Risk Management, AuditBoard, Workiva, Diligent, ServiceNow GRC.

## Source Objects

- Tax transaction, VAT return, tax code, control, control test, audit finding, remediation action, policy, risk, certification, evidence.

- Control effectiveness.
- Open findings and overdue actions.
- Tax exception analysis.
- SOX readiness.
- Audit evidence tracking.

## Output Requirements

- Include evidence retention, control owner, test result, regulatory period, materiality, and sign-off status.
