# Power BI CX Marketing Commerce Source Pack

## Vendors

Adobe Experience Cloud, Marketo, Braze, Klaviyo, Shopify, Magento/Adobe Commerce, SAP Commerce, BigCommerce, Google Analytics 4, Segment, Tealium.

## Source Objects

- Campaign, audience, contact, consent, web session, event, cart, order, product, promotion, attribution, subscription, engagement.

## Power BI Patterns

- Funnel conversion.
- Campaign ROI.
- Customer acquisition cost.
- E-commerce revenue and margin.
- Abandoned cart.
- Consent and opt-in analysis.

## Output Requirements

- Include identity stitching assumptions, consent status, attribution model, campaign hierarchy, channel, and privacy constraints.
