# Power BI Industrial KPI Benchmark Pack

Use benchmark bands to contextualize industrial KPIs without pretending they are universal truth.

## Output Contract

- Process chain, industry context, plant/company scope, and comparability assumptions.
- KPI maturity: undefined, local, governed, certified, predictive, closed-loop.
- Benchmark bands: critical, weak, stable, strong, leading.
- Improvement opportunity, financial exposure, owner, and action path.

## Checks

- State benchmark assumptions and source limitations.
- Prefer customer baseline trend when external comparability is weak.
