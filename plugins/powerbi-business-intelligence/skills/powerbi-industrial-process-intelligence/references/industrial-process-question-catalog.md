# Industrial Process Question Catalog

## Lead2Order

Typical questions:
- Which leads convert into profitable orders?
- Where do quotes stall?
- Which products, regions, or sales teams have low win rate?
- How accurate is quoted margin versus actual margin?

Core facts:
- Lead, opportunity, quote, quote item, approval, sales order item.

Core KPIs:
- Lead conversion, win rate, quote cycle time, order intake, quoted margin, discount leakage, pipeline coverage.

## Order2Cash

Typical questions:
- Why is order intake not converting to cash?
- Which orders are delayed, blocked, partially delivered, or disputed?
- What drives DSO and overdue receivables?
- Where do delivery reliability and billing accuracy fail?

Core facts:
- Sales order item, delivery item, shipment, billing item, AR open item, payment, dispute.

Core KPIs:
- Order intake, backlog, OTIF, fill rate, billing accuracy, DSO, overdue amount, dispute rate, cash conversion.

## Procure2Pay

Typical questions:
- Which suppliers cause delivery, quality, or invoice issues?
- Where is maverick buying happening?
- What drives purchase price variance?
- Which POs are stuck before goods receipt or invoice clearance?

Core facts:
- Requisition, PO item, confirmation, goods receipt, invoice receipt, payment.

Core KPIs:
- Spend, savings, PO cycle time, on-time delivery, price variance, blocked invoices, GR/IR balance, supplier risk.

## Plan2Produce

Typical questions:
- Where do production orders miss plan?
- What drives scrap, rework, yield loss, or downtime?
- Which materials constrain production?
- How do capacity bottlenecks affect delivery?

Core facts:
- Planned order, production order, operation confirmation, goods movement, quality inspection, downtime event.

Core KPIs:
- Schedule adherence, OEE, yield, scrap, rework, variance, capacity utilization, production lead time.

## Forecast2Plan

Typical questions:
- Which forecast errors drive inventory or service problems?
- Where is demand volatility increasing?
- Which products need safety stock changes?

Core facts:
- Forecast, demand signal, sales history, inventory snapshot, supply plan, capacity plan.

Core KPIs:
- Forecast accuracy, bias, MAPE, inventory coverage, service level, constrained demand, plan adherence.

## Record2Report

Typical questions:
- What drives plan/actual variance?
- Which cost centers or profit centers are off track?
- Where are close bottlenecks and late postings?

Core facts:
- Journal line, controlling posting, plan line, allocation, closing task, balance snapshot.

Core KPIs:
- Actuals, plan, variance, margin, working capital, close duration, late postings, accrual accuracy.

## Maintain2Operate

Typical questions:
- Which assets drive downtime?
- Is preventive maintenance reducing breakdowns?
- Which spare parts constrain maintenance execution?

Core facts:
- Asset, work order, notification, maintenance plan, downtime, spare part issue.

Core KPIs:
- Availability, MTBF, MTTR, planned maintenance compliance, downtime, maintenance cost, spare part fill rate.

## Issue2Resolution

Typical questions:
- Which products create warranty and quality cost?
- Where are corrective actions late?
- What drives repeat failures?

Core facts:
- Customer issue, complaint, warranty claim, quality notification, corrective action, service order.

Core KPIs:
- Claim rate, cost of poor quality, repeat issue rate, resolution time, corrective action aging, first-time fix.
