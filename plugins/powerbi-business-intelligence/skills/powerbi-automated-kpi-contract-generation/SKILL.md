# Power BI Automated KPI Contract Generation

Generate approval-ready KPI contracts.

## Output Contract

- KPI name, synonyms, business question, owner, domain, and process chain.
- Numerator, denominator, grain, date basis, filters, exclusions, currency/unit handling, and source systems.
- Validation method, tolerance, certification state, and allowed variants.
- Approval workflow and unresolved conflicts.

## Checks

- Mark generated contracts as draft until owner sign-off.
